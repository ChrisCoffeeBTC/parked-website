The Core Metric

What's a park job?

A park job is any throw that lands within 3.3 meters (11 feet) of the basket. It's the shot that sets up the easy putt. On par 4s and 5s, park jobs count on your approach shots (2nd and 3rd throws), not off the tee. Parked tracks every one so you can measure what actually matters.
